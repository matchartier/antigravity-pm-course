# Launch Timeline: TaskFlow Live (Phased Rollout)

**Total Duration:** 4 Weeks
**Approach:** "Safe Landing" then "Big Bang"

## Week 1: Enterprise Beta (The "Safe Landing")

- **Goal:** Validate stability and security with friendly power users.
- **Target:** Top 10 Enterprise Accounts (InnovateCo, TechCorp).
- **Action:**
  - Manual feature flag enablement for select workspaces.
  - White-glove onboarding webinars with AMs.
  - Daily engineering standup on performance metrics.
- **Comms:** Direct 1:1 email from Account Managers. "Early access for our best partners."

## Week 2: Limited Rollout & Polish

- **Goal:** Fix edge cases, gather testimonials.
- **Target:** Remaining Enterprise Accounts + select SMB betas.
- **Action:**
  - Enable for all "Enterprise" tier workspaces.
  - Collect quotes/logos for marketing materials.
  - Finalize marketing assets (screenshots, videos).
- **Comms:** Admin-digest email notification. Changelog update (quiet).

## Week 3: GENERAL AVAILABILITY (The "Big Bang")

- **Goal:** Maximum visibility and adoption.
- **Target:** Entire user base.
- **Action:**
  - Global feature enablement.
  - In-app tours live for all users.
  - Website homepage takeover.
- **Comms:**
  - **Email:** "TaskFlow Live is Here" blast to entire list.
  - **Social:** LinkedIn/Twitter video campaign launch.
  - **External:** Blog post & Press release live.

## Week 4: Post-Launch & Expansion

- **Goal:** Drive adoption for laggards.
- **Target:** Users who haven't tried the feature yet.
- **Action:**
  - "Did you see this?" follow-up targeting inactive segments.
  - Webinar series: "How to run remote planning with TaskFlow Live."
- **Comms:** Case study emails sharing Week 1 success stories.
