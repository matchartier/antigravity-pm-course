# Launch Campaign Plan: TaskFlow Live

**Strategy:** Enterprise-First, Big Bang Launch
**Timeline:** 4-Week Phased Rollout
**Primary Persona:** Sarah (Enterprise Admin/Buyer)

## 1. Positioning Strategy

**Core Message:** "Real-time collaboration built for the enterprise—secure, scalable, and PM-first."
**Differentiation:**

- **Vs. Linear:** "Not just for engineers—TaskFlow Live connects your entire product org."
- **Vs. Asana:** "Performance that scales with your team, not against it. Zero lag at 50+ users."
- **Vs. Monday:** "Professional grade control. Seeing is believing, but security is knowing."

## 2. Target Audience

- **Primary:** Enterprise Teams (50+ seats). _Motivation: Efficiency, Security, Compliance._
- **Secondary:** SMB/Agile Teams. _Motivation: Speed, "Modern" feel._

## 3. Simultaneous Channel Strategy ("Big Bang")

We will activate all channels simultaneously on Launch Day (Start of Week 3 GA) for maximum market noise.

**A. In-App Experience**

- **Global Banner:** "TaskFlow Live is here. See your team in real-time."
- **Feature Tour:** Auto-trigger for users on "Enterprise" plans first.
- **Empty State Nudges:** "Project is lonely. Invite your team to see cursors live."

**B. Email Campaign**

- **Enterprise Admins:** Personal outreach snippet from Account Managers. "Unlock this for your team."
- **General User Base:** "Say goodbye to refresh." animated GIF header.
- **Inactive Users:** "TaskFlow has changed. Come see what's new."

**C. External/Social**

- **Blog:** "Why we built real-time differently (WebSocket deep dive)."
- **LinkedIn:** Video clips of PM usage (not just code review).
- **PR:** TechCrunch exclusive: "TaskFlow enters the real-time era."

## 4. Success Metrics

- **Adoption:** 40% of Daily Active Users (DAU) utilizing real-time features within 30 days.
- **Retention:** Reduce churn in Enterprise accounts by 15% in Q3.
- **Performance:** Maintain <100ms latency at peak load.
